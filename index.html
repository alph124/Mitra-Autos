About Us

Mitra Autos is an automobile repair and sales shop situated in Angamaly, providing a comprehensive range of services to car owners in the region. Our shop is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of experienced mechanics, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and quality workmanship

With years of experience in the automotive industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, honesty, and excellence. We understand the importance of your vehicle in your daily life, which is why we strive to provide efficient and effective solutions to keep your car running smoothly.

At Mithra Autos, we believe in transparency and building long-term relationships with our customers. We take pride in our work and ensure that every vehicle that comes to our shop receives the attention it deserves.
